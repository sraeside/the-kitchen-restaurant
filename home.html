the kitchen is a casual and intimate italian-inspired resturaunt, specializing in fresh, handmade pasta and family-style dishes designed to share.

With the warm hospitality and energy of a traditional Italian home, we offer family-style service: all dishes are designed for sharing and the food arrives in the middle of the table, as it is prepared. The antipasti arrives first, with pasta courses close behind. We also offer house-made gluten-free options available daily. All menu items are available for dine-in or take-out.
